BEHIND THE CONCEPT OF CROWD FUND

“NOT PERMANENT”. Hope you might wonder about the story line in it or may be question me about what do I want to convey? Yup you are right, there’s no story in it, since this ‘Legendry-Saga’wouldn’t fit in a short film or any independent span in fact. Is it my story? Definitely not, this is narrated out of my personal inspiration from many lives like your's and things that might have happened or happening around the society, which is also the drive behind terming this as ‘Legendry-Saga’.

In order to film this, I could barely find the Manager’s schedule to narrate the story, let-alone finding Hero’s time. The other option is either to find the newcomers but this won’t be market viable in this case, also adding to this; Prime Investors these days are offering to act in this film, with all due respect we have rejected them. We wanted to do a Viewable Movie ethically! This fired a thought in us like “Instead of 10 sponsor investing 10 Lakh , we want to get a million viewers contributing 10 RS each". Optimistically you understand us here. Yes, we can definitely film this with your presence and we owe our tributes to you all for the same.

This Pilot project is just one percent of what we want to express. Hopefully you admire our work here, if yes; we appeal you to consider our bold-request on “Fund-Contribution”. Rest assured about our casting, technicians and their expertise; also be certain about the nostalgic moments in the movie that resemble with your own life style, of course the movie will end with giving you the joyful & proud moments. To know more about us; Contact us!

SYNOPSIS

It's a story of a guy about before maturity ( btech )& after maturity ( career ). how a boy becomes a man and gains maturity. In search of society needs , how parents and their surroundings influence children and their situations; including their love lives and what happens when his lover leads further life. On certain time or age, how impractical the life is in between fantasy & reality is the story of #NotPermanent
