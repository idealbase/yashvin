SYNOPSIS

Its a Story of a present youth before maturity & after maturity. How practically they wants to live & achieve their own life. But how parents & surroundings influencing them & their situations. based on how love life, lover leads further life. On certain time or age, how impractical the life is in between fantacy & reality is the story of #NotPermanent
